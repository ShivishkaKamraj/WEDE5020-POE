!!!STOP ANIMAL TESTING!!!

Over 110 million animals die in laboratories due to animal testing every year, enduring painful procedures as they are often immobilized, mutilated, and exposed to toxic substances.

Animal testing should be discontinued as it causes animals unnecessary pain, often fails to predict human responses accurately, and is increasingly replaced by more reliable, ethical, and cost-effective alternatives. Innovations like in vitro testing and computer modeling offer humane ways to conduct experiments, yielding better data for human health. The shift towards cruelty-free practices aligns with evolving societal values and scientific advancements.
